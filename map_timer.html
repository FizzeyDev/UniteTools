How to use the Map Timer

🗺️

Choose a map

Use the buttons at the top to switch between Groudon, Kyogre and Rayquaza maps. The timer and all spawns reset automatically.

⏱️

Control the timer

Press ▶ Play to start the countdown from 10:00. Drag the slider or edit the fields to jump to any time. Press Space to play/pause, R to reset.

🟢

Pokémon spawns

Icons appear at the correct game time. Click to mark as defeated — it greys out and the respawn timer starts automatically.

🗼

Goal zones

Click a tower to mark it destroyed. Some Pokémon only appear depending on which goals are broken, so keep them updated.

📋

Tracker

Click the Tracker button (top-right) or press T to open the kill tracker. See when mobs were killed and when they respawn. Switch between Mobs and Towers tabs.

💬

Tooltips

Hover any icon for ~1 second to see HP, spawn rules, and interactions.

⌨️

Keyboard shortcuts

Space Play/Pause  ·  R Reset  ·  T Tracker  ·  H Help  ·  Esc Close
